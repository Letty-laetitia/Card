Ambiguous
/æmˈbɪɡjuəs/
adj. 模棱两可的
Having more than one possible meaning.
The ending of the movie is ambiguous and open to interpretation.
电影的结局模棱两可，可以有不同的解释。

/bəˈnevələnt/

The benevolent old man donated most of his wealth to charity.
那位仁慈的老人将大部分财富捐给了慈善机构。
